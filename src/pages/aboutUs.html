Hello friends,

We are a small community living in the Talysh mountains in the south of Azerbaijan. We have been living in the forest for 3 years, in the house we built together with our community. Our area of about 4 hectares is located in the territory of Hirkan National Park, surrounded by mountains. Our goal in moving to these lands is to live a healthy life close to the land and nature.

Just as our community is diverse, so are the things we want to do and are mostly in the areas of health, ecology, culture and fine arts. From the new season, we will organize various art residencies, events, meetings, festivals, etc. we plan to spend.

If you want to support us in these dreams or join us, then definitely write to us.
